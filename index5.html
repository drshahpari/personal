dr abozar ashahpari

Dr. Abuzar Shahpari is the largest developer of residential towers and the president of the Iranian Elite Association. He is the author and writer of 38 book titles, as well as the writer of 4 reference books. He has made significant contributions in the field of civil engineering in Iran by utilizing artificial intelligence. Dr. Abuzar Shahpari is considered a brilliant mind and a young Iranian elite who is full of innovative engineering ideas in the fields of civil engineering, construction, architecture, urban planning, urban management, beautification, tower construction, water treatment and desalination, electricity generation, solar energy, petrochemical and refinery construction, clinics and hospitals, office and commercial complexes, and tourism centers. He uses artificial intelligence to design smart cities with renewable energy and sustainable cities.

Dr. Abuzar Shahpari, in one of his speeches at the World Engineers Conference in the United Arab Emirates, stated: "Instead of relying on manual labor and old-fashioned methods, we must move toward modern sciences and artificial intelligence, utilizing the digital world that increases precision, speed, and quality. Artificial intelligence can be widely used in the construction industry." Dr. Abuzar Shahpari has been able to bring about significant changes in three main areas of engineering projects: design and planning, execution and construction, and maintenance and operation, by using artificial intelligence.

As the founder of Building Information Modeling (BIM) in Iran, Dr. Abuzar Shahpari has provided creative and optimized solutions to design challenges using artificial intelligence, machine learning algorithms, and neural networks during the design phase.
